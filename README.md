<h1 align="center">Monarch</h1>

<p align = 'justify'>
The Monarch is the ultimate symbol of control, ruling a vast domain with unquestioned clarity and power. Our mission with Monarch is to bring that same level of absolute sovereignty to your product's financial future. In the complex world of new product launches, costs can feel like an unruly territory, ruled by chaos and guesswork spread across countless spreadsheets. We believe in replacing that chaos with command. By defining flexible **Projects**, exploring comparative **Scenarios**, and precisely modeling every granular **Component** (from fixed salaries to variable API fees), Monarch empowers you to foresee every financial outcome. Our aim is simple: to give you the clarity and foresight needed to reign supreme over your budget and launch your product not with hope, but with the unshakeable certainty of a true Monarch.
</p>
